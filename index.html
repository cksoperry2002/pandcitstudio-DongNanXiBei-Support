❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I play Destiny Fold?

A:

  • Enter your name
  • Choose a direction: North (東), South (西), East (南), or West (北)
  • Pick a number from 1-8
  • Watch the paper fold and reveal your fortune!
Q2: What are the different fortune types?

A: Destiny Fold features two types of fortunes:

  • Fortune (命運): Positive messages and guidance
  • Dare (懲罰): Fun challenges to complete
Q3: How do I share my fortune?

A:

  • After revealing your fortune
  • Tap the "Share" button
  • Choose where to share (WhatsApp, WeChat, Facebook, etc.)
  • Your friends will see your name, direction, number, and fortune
Q4: Can I see my game history?

A: Yes! Tap the "History" tab at the bottom to view all your previous fortunes. You can see:

  • Your name
  • Direction and number chosen
  • Fortune text
  • Date and time
Q5: How do I delete my history?

A:

  • Go to the History tab
  • Tap "Clear All" button
  • Confirm the deletion

Note: This action cannot be undone

Q6: Is there sound in the game?

A: Yes! The app features paper-folding sound effects. You can toggle sound on/off in the Settings tab.

Q7: Can I play with multiple people?

A: Absolutely! Destiny Fold is perfect for parties and groups. Players take turns choosing their direction and number, then share their fortunes with the group.

Q8: Is my data private?

A: Yes! All your game history is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share any personal information.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Problem: App crashes when I try to play

Solution: Try restarting the app or updating to the latest version

Problem: Sound effects are not working

Solution: Check if sound is enabled in Settings. Also ensure your device volume is not muted.

Problem: I can't share my fortune

Solution: Make sure you have the latest version. Check if you have permission to access sharing features.

Problem: History is not saving

Solution: This should not happen. Please contact us at support@pcanditstudio.com with details.
